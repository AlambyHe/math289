Qixia Temple

Qixia Temple ( simplified Chinese : 栖霞寺 ; traditional Chinese : 棲霞寺 ; pinyin : Qīxiá Sì ) is a Buddhist temple located on Qixia Mountain [1] in the suburban Qixia District of Nanjing , Jiangsu , People's Republic of China, 22 kilometres (14 mi) northeast of downtown Nanjing. [2] It is one of Nanjing's most important Buddhist monasteries. The temple is the cradle of East Asian Mādhyamaka . [3]
