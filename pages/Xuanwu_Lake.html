Xuanwu Lake

Xuanwu Lake ( Chinese : 玄武湖 ; pinyin : Xuánwǔ hú ) is located in Xuanwu District in the central-northeast part of Nanjing , Jiangsu . It is near the Nanjing Railway Station and Ji Ming Temple . [1] Five islands within the lake are interconnected by arched bridges. A visit to the lake and its park can include up to a five-hour walk. Within the park are temples, pagodas, pavilions, gardens, teahouses, restaurants, entertainment venues, a small zoo, and other attractions. Its main entrance is the Xuanwu Gate.
