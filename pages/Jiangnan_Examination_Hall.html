Jiangnan Examination Hall

The Jiangnan Examination Hall ( simplified Chinese : 江南贡院 ; traditional Chinese : 江南貢院 ; pinyin : Jiāngnán Gòngyuàn , Jiangnan Gongyuan ), near the Confucius temple, is located in the southern part of Nanjing , Jiangsu Province, China. It is the largest examination hall for imperial examination in ancient China .
