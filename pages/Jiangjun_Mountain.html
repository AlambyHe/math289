Jiangjun Mountain

Jiangjun Mountain ( Chinese : 将军山 ; pinyin : jiāngjūnshān ) is located near Tiexin Bridge in Jiangning District , Nanjing , Jiangsu Province, China . Facing Qinhuai River , it borders Niushou Mountain. Today, it is a place of historical interest.
